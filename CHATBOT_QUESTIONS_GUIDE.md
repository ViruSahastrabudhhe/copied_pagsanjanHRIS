## 💡 **TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS**

1. **Be specific**: "Find John Doe" is better than just "John"
2. **Use natural language**: The chatbot understands conversational questions
3. **Try variations**: If one phrasing doesn't work, try another
4. **Use follow-ups**: Click the suggested follow-up questions for related info
5. **Mix languages**: Feel free to use English, Tagalog, or Taglish
6. **Check details**: Click "Show Full Details" for complete information
